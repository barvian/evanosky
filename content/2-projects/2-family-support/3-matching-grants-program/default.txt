Title: Matching grants program

----

Text: 

(image: change.gif)

The Evanosky Foundation's Matching Grants program is available to organizations and community groups who are fundraising for a specific family in the midst of a medical crisis.  The Evanosky Foundation will match funds raised for a family after a plan has been made in advance to match a specific, agreed upon amount. 

Many companies match their employees' donations to charitable organizations.  You can find out if The Evanosky Foundation has partnered with your company to receive these employee-matching gifts on the (link: donate text: donation page).
